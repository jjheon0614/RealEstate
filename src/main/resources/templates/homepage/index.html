Why should you choose SHKJM as your home evaluation tool?

With the rise of technology in the recent years, many people have opted for convenience in many activities, real estate is certainly not an exception. To aid in real estate advertising and purchasing, we created SHKJM, a website that contains information of land properties in District 1 to District 12 in Ho Chi Minh City. Each property has a title, an address, the real estate type (which is a house, a villa, an apartment, or a land), the total area in square feet, the price in billion VND, the contact’s name and number so that potential customers can contact the agent or owner. If the real estate is either a house, a villa or an apartment, the property information will also include the number of floors it contains and its feature, the number of bedrooms and bathrooms it has. Since everything can be done online, both parties are allowed to communicate freely in their own time and convenience. This also allows the buyer to reach an agreement with the seller efficiently and quickly.
